We are the Mayor's Office for Innovation & Entrepreneurship.

On June 21, 2016, the Sacramento City Council created new programs to create jobs, diversify the economy, and make Sacramento a hub of innovation, technology, and entrepreneurship. Leveraging the City's Innovation & Growth Fund, the Council created a startup in City Hall - the Mayor's Office for Innovation & Entrepreneurship (MOFIE). MOFIE helps advance and implement innovative best learnings from around the country by working hand-in-hand with startups, the ecosystem, and City staff.
